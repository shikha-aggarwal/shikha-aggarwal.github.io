Thank you for a wonderful time, Huck Finn!

I was hooked on a book after a long while. In the mornings, I would get up excited, go through the chores with a longing. A longing for a window of even 15 minutes where I could gobble up some more of this story. I was even happy if my kid kicked me and woke me up in the middle of the night. Nobody needs a phone to see if some remotely curious message has popped up from no one - when you need to find out about the next pit stop of Huckleberry and Jim on the mighty river! And, when the two scammers join them, the book just runs along.

I read Tom Sawyer a few years ago and I remember I had a good time; but didn't pick up Huck Finn then because it seemed it would be about the same. Another boy, another adventure. The wait was worth it.

After finishing it, I was bummed that the whole experience had come to an end.
